Tailor-made life cover to suit your individual needs

Designed to make your life easier

Although we can’t prevent what life has in store for you, the very least we can do is make sure that your family will be provided for financially if you’re no longer able to do so.

Terminal Illness Benefit

If you’re diagnosed with a terminal illness with less than a year to live, we will pay out 50% of the total insured amount (maximum limit of … applies).

Premium based on your specific risk profile

Did you know that we base your life insurance premium on your specific risk profile? This means that if you’re a low-risk client, you’ll actually pay much less than somebody else who is a higher risk.

Flexible premium structures

We thought it best to provide you with flexible premium structures to accommodate your budget. You can choose whether to keep your premium fixed or you can increase your premium on an annual basis. You can also choose to increase both the Apex amount and the premium by a set percentage every year.
